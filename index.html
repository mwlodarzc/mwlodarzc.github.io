Bio

I obtained a B.Eng. in Automation and Robotics from Wrocław University of Science and Technology (WUST) in 2024, where I completed my thesis on Reinforcement Learning for Autonomous Drones. Building on this foundation, I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Machine Learning at Warsaw University of Technology (WUT).

My research is driven by a passion for science and engineering, focusing on developing interpretable models with large learning capacities. I am particularly interested in the science of cognition, Implicit Neural Representations and World Models.

Currently, I am part of the Center for Credible AI, where I work on XAI and Model Science projects, and the Systems Research Institute of the Polish Academy of Sciences, contributing to the DRAGONS project.
